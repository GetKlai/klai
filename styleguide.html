Rules

Rules and anti-patterns

Important constraints to maintain the Klai visual identity across all products.

Do

BackgroundsWarm ivory (#fffef2) or cream (#f3f2e7)
Text colorsUse opacity variants of rl-dark for hierarchy
Accent usage#fcaa2d as background only. #a36404 for text links.
Font weightsRegular (400) for most text. Medium (500) for titles. Bold (700) sparingly.
ImageryProduct screenshots, oil-painting backgrounds
MotionSubtle hover responses, scroll-triggered opacity only
Card radiusrounded-xl (12px). Sections: rounded-2xl (16px). Buttons: rounded-full.

Don't

Neon / gradient accentsNot Klai. Calm, not flashy.
Cold enterprise graysLoses warmth. Use cream/ivory tones.
Stock photos of peopleSays nothing about the product.
Multiple font familiesParabole handles everything. No Inter, Manrope, etc.
Accent as text color#fcaa2d fails WCAG AA on light backgrounds.
Auto-playing videoBreaks the calm aesthetic.
Decorative animationsNo shimmer, meteor, beam effects.
Many elements per screenOne point per section. Diluting = confusing.
